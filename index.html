Work

sample 1 Chopin Piano Sonata No. 2, Op. 35, I - Grave – Doppio movimento

Chopin's second piano sonata is perhaps best known for its third movement funeral march, but its other movements are beautiful as well. I play the stormy first movement. The fast pace of this piece and large chords make it technically demanding, and the lyrical sections make it musically demanding as well.

sample 2 Mozart Piano Sonata No. 13, K. 333, all three movements

I bring you almost 20 minutes of classical music - memorized! Mozart's 13th piano sonata is typical of his music, exploring a wide variety of playful themes. I find the large number of rapid scales in the third movement (11:26) the most exciting aspect of this piece.

sample 3 Chopin Ballade No. 1, Op. 23

Perhaps the best known usage of Chopin's first Ballade is in the film The Pianist, when the protagonist plays it for the Nazi officer that discovers him. Here, I play the piece for you in full. The ballade covers all sorts of themes, from tragic to agitated to playful. Blazing octaves and scales add technical difficulty. This is another work that takes much prowess to play.
